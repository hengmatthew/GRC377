The Gunbuddy Boys


Bringing your in-game keychains to life.

Gun Buddies are little charms that can be attached to one’s in-game weapon for purely aesthetic reasons. In essence, these gun buddies are just keychains for your gun. Gun Buddy Boys is all about making them into real life physical keychains you can actually hold.
